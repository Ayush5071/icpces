How to reach

Allahabad (Prayagraj) is well-connected by road, rail and air. Daily trains, buses and flights are available from major Indian cities. For travel planning, please consult your travel agent or visit transport provider websites for schedules and ticketing.

About the city

The city is a primary center for culture, religion and education. The confluence of Ganga, Yamuna and mythical Saraswati rivers defines this city. Many national and regional headquarters of administration, military and research facilities are located in the city.

Historical note

Allahabad has produced several prime ministers and prominent leaders and is home to notable institutions like Allahabad University and historic sites such as Sangam (confluence), All Saints Cathedral, Alfred Park, Khusro Bagh, and more.

Tourist attractions

Sangam (confluence of rivers), All Saints Cathedral, Alfred Park, Khusro Bagh, Yamuna Bridge, Public Library (est. 1864), Allahabad University (est. 1887), etc.
